About Me

I am a second year PhD student focusing on Graph Representation Learning, Graph Neural Networks, and Meta-Learning. Before following my passion for research, I gained some industry experience with a six months internship developing Deep Learning algorithms for digital pathology, and spending 2 months as a Machine Learning consultant. I have a broad interest in Deep Learning, and I'm extremely passionate about it. I am currently an intern at Samsung AI Research under the supervision of Efi Tsamoura and Timothy Hospedales.
